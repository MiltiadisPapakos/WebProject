Upload your data!















In order to add your data click the button "Browse" and select a .json file downloaded from google maps. Then click the button "Upload" to upload your data. All data which are outside of the city of Patras will be automatically removed. In addition if you want all the data from a specific area to be removed create a rectangle in this area.
To create a rectangle: click on the map, move the mouse on the map, click again when you have created the rectangle you want. You can create more than one rectangles. With the button "Undo" you can erase the last rectangle you created. With the button "Reset" you can erase all rectangles.
